My Journey

My journey into the world of cybersecurity started in a curious way, at a very young age. I have always been fascinated by how things work behind the scenes of systems, and this led me to penetration testing (pentesting). Today, I am in the early stages of an intense and challenging path where I am learning the tools and techniques that are essential for a professional pentester.

Currently, I am dedicating myself to studying advanced pentesting techniques, vulnerability hunting, and testing system security on platforms like TryHackMe and Hack The Box. While I'm just starting out, every new challenge is an opportunity to learn and grow. I am focused on improving my skills in areas such as networking, web application security, and automation of security testing.

At the same time, I am getting involved with the cybersecurity community by participating in training, workshops, and sharing knowledge through blogs and technical discussions. I know the road is long and full of challenges, but I’m excited about what lies ahead, and every small progress motivates me to keep going. My goal is to help strengthen the security of organizations and, hopefully, contribute to major innovations in the field someday.
